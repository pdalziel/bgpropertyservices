B & G Property Services Ltd

Commercial & Residential Maintenance

Established in 2015, B&G Property Services have quickly become one of Wellington’s up and coming building companies, with a reputation for reliable and high-quality work. We offer a comprehensive range of domestic and commercial building services, ranging from door stops and smoke alarms to kitchen and bathroom remodelling. Much of our work comes from repeat business from existing clients and recommendations, which is testament to the high standard of work we provide. When you choose B&G Property Services, you can trust that our staff will be respectful of the environment we work within and will have a highly professional attitude at all times.

We hold full public liability insurance (up to $10 000 000) and work with the HazardCo system to ensure our customers peace of mind when using our services. We offer an emergency call out service to all our customers. We offer free quotes 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We are fully qualified, and only employ qualified staff.

We cover Wellington, Lower Hutt, Porirua, Kapiti, Upper Hutt, Masterton, Otaki & more.
